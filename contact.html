Good reasons to reach out

  • You want to discuss a job or internship opportunity
  • You want help thinking through a tech issue or setup
  • You would like feedback on a portfolio, website, or digital presentation
  • You want to ask about a project, article topic, or future collaboration

Helpful things to include

  • What you are trying to do or fix
  • Any deadline, class, or work context that matters
  • What you have already tried so far
  • Whether you want advice, hands-on help, or direction on next steps

Q and A

Before you send a message

What kind of messages fit this website best?

Anything related to projects, opportunities, technical questions, portfolio feedback, or support conversations fits well here.

Is this page only for formal business contact?

No. It can be professional, but it is also for approachable conversations about tech, advice, and problem solving.

What if I am not sure how to describe the issue?

That is okay. A simple explanation of what is not working or what you are trying to achieve is enough to start.

One message can open the door

The goal is to make reaching out feel easy, not intimidating.

That is part of the same people-first approach behind the rest of the site.
